About BootCamp

Welcome to our coding bootcamp! Our goal is to help you develop your coding skills, increase your awareness of the importance of coding, and land your dream job. We believe that everyone, regardless of their background, has the potential to succeed. That's why we have designed this program specifically for Tier 3 college students. You will learn problem-solving techniques and have access to a supportive community. Let's get started on this exciting journey together. We have faith in your potential and are here to support you every step of the way!
